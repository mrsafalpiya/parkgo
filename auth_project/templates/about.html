About Us

Welcome to Park and Go, where simplicity meets convenience in the world of parking solutions. Established on March 1, 2024, by Lakhe Company in the heart of Kathmandu, Park and Go was founded with a singular mission – to make parking simpler and easier for everyone.

At Park and Go, we bridge the gap between drivers in search of parking and individuals with available parking spaces. Our platform serves as the ultimate connector, facilitating a seamless experience for both parties. We take pride in our commitment to transforming the parking landscape, offering innovative solutions that prioritize user ease and accessibility.

Since our inception, Park and Go has become the go-to choice for over 10 thousand drivers who now enjoy the benefits of hassle-free parking. Join us on this journey as we continue to redefine and elevate the parking experience for individuals and communities alike. At Park and Go, simplicity and convenience are not just goals – they are our commitment to making every parking experience a breeze.
