About Us

Welcome to ParkGo, where simplicity meets convenience in the world of parking solutions. Established on March 1, 2024, by Lakhe Company in the heart of Kathmandu, ParkGo was founded with a singular mission – to make parking simpler and easier for everyone.

At ParkGo, we bridge the gap between drivers in search of parking and individuals with available parking spaces. Our platform serves as the ultimate connector, facilitating a seamless experience for both parties. We take pride in our commitment to transforming the parking landscape, offering innovative solutions that prioritize user ease and accessibility.

Since our inception, ParkGo has become the go-to choice for over 10 thousand drivers who now enjoy the benefits of hassle-free parking. Join us on this journey as we continue to redefine and elevate the parking experience for individuals and communities alike. At ParkGo, simplicity and convenience are not just goals – they are our commitment to making every parking experience a breeze.
